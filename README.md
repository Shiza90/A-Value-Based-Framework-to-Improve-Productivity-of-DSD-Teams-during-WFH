# A-Value-Based-Framework-to-Improve-Productivity-of-DSD-Teams-during-WFH
This repository contains the supporting calculations and tables used in this research work. Title of the work is: A Value-Based Framework to Improve Productivity of DSD Teams during WFH 

**>Objective**
To identify and validate the challenges of DSD during WFH. 
Identify, evaluate, and rank critical challenges that affect productivity in DSD environments while WFH using a structured multi-criteria decision-making (MCDM) approach.
Evaluate the effectiveness of proposed Value-Aligned Framework.

**>Methodology**
Literature review to identify and industrial survey to validate the DSD challenges during WFH.
VBSE for systematic identification of value-critical challenges.
Bipolar Fuzzy Logic to capture nuanced expert judgments.
CRITIC to derive objective weights based on inter-criteria correlation.
CODAS to rank challenges with consideration of both Euclidean and Taxicab distances.
Expert judgement to validate the proposed framework.

**>Privacy Statement**
